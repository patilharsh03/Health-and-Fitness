Health and Fitness

Health means as a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity, whereas Fitness means a set of attributes that people have or achieve that relates to the ability to perform physical activity.

Why we need to be Fit and Healthy?

Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like type 2 diabetes, cancer and cardiovascular disease. Physical activity and exercise can have immediate and long-term health benefits. Most importantly, regular activity can improve your quality of life.

Imapct on Brain

Exercise affects the brain in many ways. ... It aids the release of hormones which provide an excellent environment for the growth of brain cells. Exercise also promotes brain plasticity by stimulating growth of new connections between cells in many important cortical areas of the brain.

Benefits in Mental Health

Participation in regular physical activity can increase our self-esteem and can reduce stress and anxiety. It also plays a role in preventing the development of mental health problems and in improving the quality of life of people experiencing mental health problems.
